GRANNY'S LEMON PIE

INGREDIENTS

1 cup white sugar
2 tbsp all-purpose flour
3 tbsp cornstarch
1/4 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups water
2 lemons, juiced and zested
2 tbsp butter
4 egg yolks, beaten
1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
